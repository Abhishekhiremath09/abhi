About Tractors

A Massey tractorDI Sona tractor has a 3 cylinder engine that produces 44 HP with 37.84 PTO HP. Moreover, this Massey Ferguson tractor engine capacity is 2500 CC. The Massey Ferguson 244 DI Sona gearbox has 10 Forward + 2 Reverse gears and 2 WD, which makes the performance reliable.

Types of massey:

  • Massey Fergusion 241di – 42HP
  • Massey Fergusion 1035 – 40HP
  • Massey Fergusion 246 – 46HP
  • Massey Fergusion 8055 – 55HP

A tractor is a motor-driven vehicle designed for pulling or pushing machinery, especially in agriculture and construction
