About Swaraj

The XT Swaraj 744 XT tractor price in India starts from 7,39,000 to 7,95,000. The 744 XT tractor has a 3 cylinders engine that produces 45 HP with 44 PTO HP. Moreover, this Swaraj tractor engine capacity is 3478 CC. The Swaraj 744 XT gearbox has 8 forward + 2 reverse gears and 2 Wheel Drive

Types of Swaraj:

  • Swaraj 744xt – 44HP
  • Swaraj 742xt – 42HP
  • Swaraj 735FEE – 40HP
  • Swaraj 855FE – 50HP
